We are excited to share our [preprint](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41648200/) on making targeting mutations in satellite DNAs using a sequential CRISPR. This approach allows us to manipulate the satellite without interfering with flanking regions. We're using this approach to study the functional and evolutionary consequences of satellite DNAs. Here, we demonstrate that these mutations alter sensitivity to meiotic drive.
