Check out our new [pre-print](https://doi.org/10.64898/2026.05.27.728298) on satellite DNA evolution across phylogenetic scales! We designed a pipline to annotate and identify homologous repeats--a challenging task in non-model organisms. This helped us reveal patterns of repeat evolution within and across insect orders.
